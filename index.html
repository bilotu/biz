WE BUILD FOR THE FUTURE

Company Profile

Bilotu World Ent was incorporated in 2015 to carry out construction of Civil, Roadways, Buildings, Carpentry, Mechanical and Electrical Engineering works and the Sales/Supply of all types of building materials.

The Company has total capability in the design, procurement and delivery of major projects across a full spectrum of global markets. Guided by principles of simplicity and clarity, we strive to operate as a streamlined group of connected businesses able to transform ourselves whenever a better way is presented.

We specialise in delivering ambitious yet achievable construction projects that delight both clients and communities across Nigeria.
